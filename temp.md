$$
kT(t)=\frac{2}{d}\frac{K(t)}{N}=\frac{1}{Nd}\sum_{i=1}^Nm_i\mathbf{v}_i(t)\cdot\mathbf{v}_i(t)
$$

for two dimentional
$$
kT=\frac{1}{2N}\sum m_i\overline{\mathbf{v}_i\cdot \mathbf{v}_i}
$$
